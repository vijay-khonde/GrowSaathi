About Smart Crop Advisor

Smart Crop Advisor is an intelligent agriculture decision support system designed to assist modern farmers and students in adopting sustainable, high-yield agricultural practices.

By combining local soil guidelines, crop characteristics, and weather parameters with advanced generative artificial intelligence, the platform generates point-wise advice to optimize field preparation, sowing, fertilizing, and crop protection.

Our Technology Stack

The system has been engineered to support fast, secure full-stack transactions:

Node.js Express.js MongoDB & Mongoose Google Gemini AI Bootstrap 5 HTML5 & Vanilla JS
Hashed Authentication & Security: Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt and credentials are validated using a MongoDB user account database, shielding Gemini keys inside a secure node environment.
Core Objectives
  • Help farmers optimize input fertilization costs.
  • Facilitate natural nitrogen accumulation using legume rotations.
  • Deliver quick diagnostics of plant disease via AI image detection.
  • Promote digitized, smart-farming methodologies.
Farming Features
  • Soil Moisture Techniques (Drip, Sprinkler, Olla tips)
  • Indian region-specific crop scheduling and planner
  • Nutritional and dietary profile of staple Indian grains
  • Recent activity dashboard log retrieval
